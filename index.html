Our Story

Meet Our Founder

Pablo Hurtado

Founder and C.E.O.

With more than fifteen years in the healthcare industry, I have had the privilege of leading high-performing teams, building lasting strategic partnerships, and driving meaningful growth across organizations large and small. My background in business development, operations, and client retention has shaped a clear understanding of what truly defines great care: consistency, compassion, and a relentless focus on the people being served.

Over the years, I saw both the gaps and the possibilities within home care, and I knew there was room to do it differently. That conviction inspired me to launch Ally In-Care, a licensed home care agency built around one simple idea: that exceptional care begins with putting the client first.

Today, I bring that experience and that passion into every part of our work. My goal is not only to support the individuals and families who trust us, but to build a service rooted in integrity, professionalism, and genuine community connection, one home, one relationship, one day at a time.
